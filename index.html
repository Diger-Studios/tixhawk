What are last-minute ticket alerts? Last-minute ticket alerts are instant notifications sent to your phone when concert tickets, sports tickets, or event tickets become available at reduced prices or when new inventory is released. TixHawk specializes in finding these opportunities before they're gone.

How do ticket alert services work? Ticket alert services like TixHawk continuously monitor ticket platforms for price changes, new releases, and availability updates. Several factors cause last-minute ticket price drops: venues release held tickets, scalpers reduce prices to avoid losses, weather concerns affect outdoor events, and organizers sometimes release additional inventory.
