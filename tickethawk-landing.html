Frequently Asked Questions

How fast are the alerts really?
Our proprietary system checks ticket platforms every 30 seconds, detecting price changes 4-6 minutes before other alert services. This speed advantage is crucial for securing the best deals before they're gone.
Which ticket platforms do you monitor?
We monitor all major ticket platforms including Ticketmaster, StubHub, Vivid Seats, SeatGeek, and 20+ other verified resellers. We only alert you to legitimate tickets from trusted sources.
Can I set different price alerts for different events?
Yes! You can customize price thresholds for each artist, team, or venue you track. Set higher limits for must-see events and lower limits for casual interests.
What if I don't want alerts at certain times?
You can set quiet hours in your account settings. We'll hold non-urgent alerts until your preferred time window. Exceptional deals (70%+ off) can optionally bypass quiet hours.
Is there really no commitment?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings with one click. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. Plus, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new users.
How many alerts will I receive?
It depends on your settings. Most users receive 3-5 relevant alerts per week. You control the frequency by adjusting your price thresholds and tracked events.
Do you sell my phone number?
Never. Your phone number is used solely for sending you ticket alerts. We don't share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What happens after my free trial?
Your free trial lasts 7 days with full access to all features. After that, you'll be charged the plan you selected (monthly or yearly). You can cancel anytime during the trial without being charged.
